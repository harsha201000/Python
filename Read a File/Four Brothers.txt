
The Four Brothers Story

Once upon a time in a village, there lived 4 brothers. One day, their dad told them to do work. The four brothers did fishing, learned how to use binoculars, archery, pottery, shooting eggs and cracking eggs. In archery, One brother shooted the deer, bird, and eggs. The eggs are cracked open and they did pottery. The 4 brothers went to the palace by a boat and saw a queen. One of the brother wants to marry a queen. They lived happily ever after.
