The Sow and The Wolf

The sow had just farrowed and layed into the sty with her whole liter of pigs. A Wolf secertly made a meal on one of them, but knew not how to compass it. Sow had a good opinion and accordingly came up to her- How does this woman do today? He says. Can I be any service of you, Ms.Sow in relation to your little family here? If you have a mind to go aboard , and air yourself on little or so, you may depend on it I will take care as much as the little pigs as you could do yourself- Your humble servant says the Sow, I understand your meaning and to let you know i do. I would be free to tell you  I have rather have your room in yoir company. The sow begged the wolf.
