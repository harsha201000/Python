

Title: GUI Restaurant Management App

Description:

Our GUI Restaurant Management App is designed to help restaurant owners manage their business efficiently. With an easy-to-use graphical interface, this app allows restaurant staff to handle various tasks seamlessly.

Key Features:

Menu Management: Create, update, and customize menus effortlessly. Add new dishes, edit prices, and categorize items for easy navigation.

Order Management: Take orders swiftly and accurately. Input customer orders, modify them if needed, and track the status of each order in real-time.

Table Reservation: Manage table reservations hassle-free. Check table availability, reserve tables for customers, and keep track of reservation timings.

Inventory Tracking: Keep tabs on your inventory levels to ensure ingredients are stocked. Receive alerts for low stock items and generate reports for inventory analysis.

Billing and Payments: Streamline the billing process. Calculate bills, apply discounts if applicable, and accept various payment methods including cash, card, or digital payments.

Employee Management: Manage staff schedules, roles, and responsibilities. Keep track of employee attendance and performance.

Customer Feedback: Gather feedback from customers to improve service quality. Allow customers to rate their experience and leave comments for further insights.

Our GUI Restaurant Management App aims to simplify restaurant operations, enhance customer satisfaction, and boost overall efficiency. Whether you run a small café or a fine dining establishment, this app is tailored to meet your restaurant management needs.
